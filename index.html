Features

  • Programs
  • Calendar
  • Weight
  • Tips

Create, edit and share your own workout programs. Set goals to strive for and create a program to progress towards that goal. Our intuitive algorithm will create programs based on the results of all current users.

A weekly or monthly overview of all your workout programs. Workout programs you signed up for will show up automatically in your personalized calendar. This calendar can be linked to your phone to stay motivated.

Keep track of your weight throughout the year. Our algorithm will find patterns based on your weight throughout the week, month, year or longer when you log it. The app gives insights and advice to stay on the desired path for optimal weight whether you want to lose fat, gain muscle or just maintain.

The app's algorithm will give detailed insights about your workout results. It will send simple tips about every aspect of healthy living based on your own results.
